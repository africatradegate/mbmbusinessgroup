    About Us

    History

    Awelkare AdemImport and Export founded in 2017 by its owner, Mr. Munir, has rapidly become a dynamic player in the business landscape.

    Specializing in a diverse array of sectors, the company has carved a niche for itself, particularly excelling in the export of agricultural products. With a keen focus on quality and reliability, MBM Business Group has extended its reach into the global market, showcasing a comprehensive portfolio that spans fruits, vegetables, meat products, spices, and various related commodities. Beyond our commitment to delivering superior products,

    mbmbusinessgroup is also dedicated to sustainable and ethical business practices. We actively engage in responsible sourcing, promoting environmentally friendly practices, and maintaining fair and transparent relationships with its suppliers and partners. The company's commitment to excellence is evident in its rigorous quality control measures, ensuring that only the finest products meet the stringent standards set by international markets. This dedication to quality has not only fostered trust among clients but has also propelled the company to the forefront of the export industry.
